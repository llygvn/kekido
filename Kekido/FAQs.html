Frequently Asked Questions

What is the starting price for a custom cake?

Our custom cakes start at ₱2,000 for a 6-inch single-tier cake. Pricing depends on size, tiers, flavors, and custom decorations.

How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend placing your order at least 5-7 days in advance to ensure availability. For highly customized cakes, we suggest ordering at least two weeks ahead.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery within Dagupan City for an additional fee. Delivery fees vary based on location.

Can I customize my cake design?

Absolutely! You can upload a reference image in our order form, and we will do our best to replicate your design.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept GCash and cash payments for walk-in orders. Full payment or a 50% downpayment is required for custom orders.

Do you offer eggless or gluten-free cakes?

Yes, we offer eggless and gluten-free options upon request.
