Frequently Asked Questions

What is the starting price for a custom cake?

Our custom cakes start at ₱2,000 for a 6-inch single-tier cake. Pricing depends on size, tiers, flavors, and custom decorations.

What is the shelf life of your items?

For bread, it is best consumed within the day of purchase but it can be stored in the chiller up to 2 days. For cakes, you may store it in the chiller for 2-3 days or in the freezer for up to 7 days. For pastries, you may store it in the chiller for 2-3 days.

Can I choose the delivery date and time?

Yes, you may select your preferred delivery date upon checkout. If you have a specific time requested, you may indicate it on the special instructions.
